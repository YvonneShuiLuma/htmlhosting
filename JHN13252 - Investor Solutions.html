Description

BMO Callable Contingent Income Notes are an investment solution designed to provide investors with a predictable Coupon payment while offering partial downside protection against a decline in the Reference Asset over the term of the Notes. The Reference Asset could be an index, exchange-traded funds, basket of securities or other asset. The Notes may be suitable for income-oriented investors with a neutral to slightly bearish view of the Reference Asset over the term of the Notes. The AutoCall feature provides an opportunity to reinvest if the Reference Assets performs “better-than-expected” during the term.
